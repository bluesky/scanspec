technical terms¶

Axis: A fixed reference that can be scanned. i.e. a motor, time or number of repetitions.

Dimension: A repeatable, possibly snaking structure of frames along a number of axes.

Frame: A vector of three points in scan space: lower, midpoint, upper (used for fly scanning).

Path: A consumable route through one or more dimensions.

Point: A single or multidimensional location in scan space.

Spec: A serializable representation of the type, parameters and axis names required to produce one or more dimensions.
